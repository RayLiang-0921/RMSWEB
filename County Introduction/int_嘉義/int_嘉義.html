Chiayi
Food:
蚵仔(Oyster) Because there are several port,so the seafood here is very fresh to eat.
火雞肉飯(Turkey Rice) Needless to say, it is the most popular cuisine in Chiayi. The special texture of the turkey is so good that lots of tourists come here for it.
Attractions:
東石漁人碼頭(Dongshi Fisherman's Wharf) Lots of people come here to take some beautiful picture, you can also eat some fresh seafood here.
阿里山(Alishan) Enjoy the atmosphere there, absorb some fendozin while climbing.
Transport: Taoyuan Airport →→ Normal Bus →→ Taiwan High Speed ​​Rail →→ Chiayi
