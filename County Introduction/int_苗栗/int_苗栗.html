Miaoli
Food:
大湖草莓(Dahu strawberry) It is a kind of traditional dessert in Taiwan, the flavor is very unique,it can be dry or wet.
客家料理(Hakka Cuisine) Not just this dish, Hakka dishes are various. Like Kakka salted pork, crispy large intestine.
Attractions:
龍騰斷橋(Long Teng Broken Bridge) It is one of the historical spot in Taiwan, make us think of the old years...
竹南啤酒廠(Jhunan Brewery) If you are a drinker, it must be the best part of trip for you...
Transport: Taoyuan Airport →→ National High Way Bus →→ Train/High speed Rail →→ Miaoli
