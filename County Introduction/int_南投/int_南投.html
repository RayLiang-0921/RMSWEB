Nantou
Food:
麻糬(Mochi) It's one of the most popular souvenirs here, because it is chewy and has a good taste.
地瓜(Sweet potato) In Taiwan, Nantou is a county that produce a great amount of crops, and sweet potato here is soft and sweet of course.
Attractions:
日月潭(Sun Moon Lake) You can ride a bike around the lake, it takes you about two hours, there are also lots of stands to buy some good things.
杉林溪(Sunlinksea) Enjoy the atmosphere there, absorb some fendozin while climbing.
Transport: Taoyuan Airport →→ Normal Bus →→ Taiwan High Speed ​​Rail →→ Taichung →→ Bus →→ Nantou
