Hualien
Food:
扁食(wonton) Different to dumpling, it often be added into soup or fried with red chili, special to eat.
Attractions:
太魯閣國家公園(Taroko National Park) Taroko National Park is a mix of extraordinary sights, you can see the uncanny workmanship of the landsacpes, , if you come to Taiwan, it is the best place to visit.
秀姑巒溪(Xiuguluan River) If you are a perswon who want some exciting, dangerous, passionate activities, then white water rafting is going to be the best for you, and Xiuguluan River is a good spot to do this kind of sport.
Transport: Taoyuan Airport →→ Bus/MRT →→ Taipei Train Station →→ Hualien
