Keelung
Food:
鼎邊銼(tiánn-pinn sô、potside sticker soup) Cook the soy milk with tripod, after moments, the soy milk will be solid, cutting it into small pieces, then add some more ingredients, like shrimp, mushroom, squid etc.at last add some delicious soup into it.
吉古拉(Chikuwa) Originally from Japan.Turn the fish meat into surimi, then string it on the iron tube to grill it.
Attractions:
廟口夜市(miaokou night market) Because of the plenty of temples, lots of people gather at here and start business. AS time past, it become a night market. due to the good dishes there,it become more and more popular.
忘憂谷(Wangyougu) I have no comments for this kind of scene,it is so beautiful beyond description.
基隆海洋廣場(Keelung Maritime Plaza) It become a new attraction of Keelung, because of the incredible noght view.
Transport: Taoyuan Airport →→ the MRT of the airport →→ Taipei Bus/Train Station →→ Keelung
New Taipei
Food:
阿婆鐵蛋(Grandma Iron egg) If you want to make it, it'll waste you lots of time,so just go to buy it.The most popular iorn egg in Taiwan is this.
臭豆腐(stinky Tofu) Needless to say, it's the most famous Taiwaness food in the world, but I bet you won't eat it because foreigner can't afford it...
Attractions:
九份(miaokou night market) It's the famous for it's ancient buildings,and the unique characteristic.
野柳(yehliu) It is known for the "Queen Head", but not only this is unique,others are also worth to see.
Transport: Taoyuan Airport →→ the MRT/bus of the airport →→ Taipei Train Station →→ Taipei MRT →→ New Taipei City
Taipei
Food:
寧夏夜市(Ningxia Night Market) All the food ther is worth to try, so it is the big collection of the taiwaness food.
饒河夜市(Roahe Night Market) Same as Ningxia
Attractions:
北投溫泉(Beitou Hot Spring) I think it will be the most relaxing attraction for you because of the hot spring.
台北101(Taipei 101) The most popular attraction in Taiwan, there are lots of fun to have, because it is a kind of department store , but th scene is also beautiful.
Transport: Taoyuan Airport →→ the MRT of the airport →→ Taipei
