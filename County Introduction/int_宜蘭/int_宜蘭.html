Yilan
Food:
鴨賞(Smoked Duckling(Ya Shang)) Just like its name, it is smoked, so the special taste will surprise you. it is a little bit salty, so eat it with somrthing else may be the good choice.
三星蔥油餅(scallion pancake) It is very crispy and oily to eat, sometimes we add eggs in it with some sauce, but the most speciality is scallion, the strong flavor will definitely surprise you.
Attractions:
烏石港(Wushi Harbor) Though it is the harbor, because of the accumulation of sand, it become a good attraction to surf. Sometimes, whales appear there.
礁溪溫泉(Xiao Liuqiu) It is one of the spots in Taiwan that you can take hot spring, it doesn't have the keen scent of sulfur, so you can have lots of joy.
Transport: Taoyuan Airport →→ Normal Bus →→ Yilan
