Matsu
Food:
紅糟(Red Vinasse) It is a kind of seasoning that can added in different dishes, and it is benefit for your health.
地瓜餃(Sweet Potato Dumplings) The wrapper of the stuffing is made of sweet potato, several dumplings will make you full because of the sweet potaoes.
Attractions:
芹壁村落(Qinbi Village) This village was built in mountainside, the buildings are very different to the normal houses in Taiwan, worthy to visit.
