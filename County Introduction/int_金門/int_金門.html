Kinmen
Food:
金門高粱酒(Kinmen Kaoliang (Sorghum) Liquor) It is the most famous liquor in Taiwan, the smell of sorghum is fragrant, and be careful, it is the high-strentgh liquor, so the taste is spicy.
貢糖(Peanut Candy) Just like its name, it is made of peanut, sugar, and maltose. It is a graet souvenir that can prove you have been to Kinmen.
Attractions:
太武山(Taiwu Mountain) It is the highest mountain in Kinmen, In the peroid of Second Taiwan Strait Crisis, it plays a significant role to help the soldiers resist the enemies.
大膽島(Dadan Islands) Kinmen is an historical island that is full of traces of war, and Dadan Islands used to be the military base, so you can find out the history of Taiwan in person.
