Kaohsiung
Food:
岡山肉羊爐(Gangshan's lamb hot pot) If you are not a big fan of mutton, then it is not your type, but if you are, the strong flavor of it is so good.
丹丹漢堡(Dain-Dain Hamburger) It is the local fast food restaurant in Taiwan, it provide not only hanburger and other ordinary stuff but also some local food, like fried rice cake, thickened souo with meat.
Attractions:
駁二(The Pier-2) A lot of public art are displayed here, sometimes differents will come here to sell their goods here, it is a good place to come.
愛河(Love River) The nights of Love River are very charming, and there are some tourist guys to take you to know the beautiful history that Kaohsiung.
Transport: Taoyuan Airport →→ Normal Bus →→ Taiwan High Speed ​​Rail →→ Kaohsiung
