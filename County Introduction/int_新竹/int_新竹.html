Hsinchu
Food:
擂茶(mashed tea) It is a kind of traditional dessert in Taiwan, the flavor is very unique,it can be dry or wet.
貢丸(pork ball) The balls that made of pork, juicy. Rich Flavors is the feature of it.
Attractions:
17公里海岸線(17km coastline) You can ride a bike by this, and the view will get you shocked, best thing ever.
內灣老街(Neiwan old street) The Hakka culture is the unique point at this street, and with the bridge, natural scenes. You will be enjoyed there.
Transport: Taoyuan Airport →→ National High Way Bus →→ Train/High speed Rail →→ Hsinchu
